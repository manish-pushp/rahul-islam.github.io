Yuvaan 2k16

Yuvaan, the annual technical and cultural extravaganza hosted by IIIT Guwahati during February is a time of celebration, enjoyment and recreation.

Every Year when our campus is energized with the spirit of people and ideas, and springs up with momentum, the latent energy awakens to manifest itself into magnificence. The cultural, technical and managerial spirits mingle to bring forth the Fizz, Frolic and Festivity of this extravaganza.

The students of the Institute come together to create an event that is the expression of their creativity and technical prowess, from hosting technical contests to mesmerizing nights and music, from digital wars to equally ferocious brush strokes, from the laidback literary congregations to high octane adventure sports, and the cognitive memory based Quizzes. This is just a small glimpse. IIIT Guwahati

IIIT Guwahati, one among the newly setup IIITs had come onto existence on 18 March 2013. The Institute is operating from the heart of Guwahati, with cozy infrastructure for the small IIIT Family, and with state-of-art laboratory equipment. It is in the Assam Textile Institute campus. The Permanent campus is Under Construction and the IIIT-G family hope to move to the campus in the middle of 2017. IIIT Guwahati is indeed having a Humble beginning, but believes in the fact that the Universe was once just cosmic dust. Industry-academia relationship is given top priority
